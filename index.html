How to test the voice AI

Option 1 — Call from your browser: Click the "📞 Call AI in browser" button in the top right. Your browser will ask for mic permission (one time), then connect you directly to the AI. A live transcript appears below the header during the call.

Option 2 — Real phone call: Dial +1 (930) 254-9264. The AI greets you with "Hello, you've reached the RTL Business Assistant."

Option 3 — Verify the data directly: Each section below has a live tester that hits the same backend endpoint the voice AI uses. If the tester returns the right data, the AI can use it on a call.

Cold-start note: The backend sleeps after 15 min of no traffic on Render's free tier. The first request after sleep takes ~30 seconds to wake up. The voice AI tools are set to wait 45 seconds, but if you hear a long pause on a call, that's why.
